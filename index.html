About Me

I am currently an associate professor in the School of Information at Huazhong Agricultural University. I was a Post-Doctoral Fellow at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, advised by Prof. Bo Li and Prof. Shaohuai Shi from 2023-2024. I was also a Post-Doctoral Fellow at Hong Kong Polytechnic University, advised by Prof. Jiannong Cao from 2024-2025. I received my Bachelor and Master degrees from Wuhan University of Technology in 2016 and 2019; and my PhD degree from Wuhan University in 2023, advised by Prof. Ruiting Zhou and Prof. Zongpeng Li.

I am now looking for graduate students and intern students who are interested in machine learning systems, LLM training and inference, distributed learning, and cloud/edge computing. If you have interests, feel free to contact me.
